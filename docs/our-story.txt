Konkani Kutam (meaning "Konkani Family") was founded in 2003 by a small group of Konkani Catholic families  who felt the familiar longing, the longing for community, for the sound of Konkani in the air, for the smell of Monthi Fest flowers, and for the warmth of a church that felt like home.

Today we are a vibrant, growing association connecting over 100 members from cities including Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin, Stuttgart, Cologne, Hamburg, Düsseldorf and beyond. We are registered as a non-profit association (eingetragener Verein – e.V.) in Germany, governed by an elected committee of volunteers passionate about our heritage.

We are descendants of the Konkani speaking Catholics of the Konkan coast, whose culture is a beautiful blend of Goan-Portuguese, Mangalorean, Indian, and now German influences. We carry that story forward with joy.
