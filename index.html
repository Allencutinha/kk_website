About Konkani Kutam

Who we are, what we stand for, and the people who make this community thrive.

Our Story

Konkani Kutam (meaning "Konkani Family") was founded in 2015 by a small group of Mangalorean Catholic families scattered across Germany who felt the familiar longing — the longing for community, for the sound of Konkani in the air, for the smell of Monthi Fest flowers, and for the warmth of a church that felt like home.

Today we are a vibrant, growing association connecting over 200 members from cities including Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin, Stuttgart, Cologne, Hamburg, Düsseldorf and beyond. We are registered as a non-profit association (eingetragener Verein – e.V.) in Germany, governed by an elected committee of volunteers passionate about our heritage.

We are proudly Mangalorean — descendants of the Catholics of the Konkan coast, whose ancestors were baptised centuries ago and whose culture is a beautiful blend of Goan-Portuguese, Indian, and now German influences. We carry that story forward with joy.

At Konkani Kutam, it is our biggest annual event — a full day of Holy Mass, cultural performances, traditional food, and the timeless ritual of the first harvest offering. Our Initiatives

Beyond events, we run year-round programmes that nurture our culture, support our families, and strengthen our community.

🗣️

Konkani Language Classes

Monthly online and in-person classes for children and adults. From basic conversational Konkani to reading the Devanagari script — because every generation should be able to speak the language of their ancestors. Taught by volunteer teachers from within our community.

🎵

Cultural Programmes & Arts

Dance, drama, music, and poetry in Konkani. We train children and youth in traditional Konkani folk dances, Konkani folk songs (Mando, Dulpod), and stage performances at our annual events — keeping our performing arts alive in Germany.

🤝

Newcomer Welcome Programme

Just arrived in Germany? We connect you with a buddy from our community who helps you navigate registration, insurance, schools, healthcare, and the unwritten rules of German life — in Konkani or English, whichever feels like home.

👶

Konkani Kids Club

A dedicated space for children of our community to meet, play, and grow up knowing their heritage. Monthly gatherings with storytelling, crafts, Konkani rhymes, and activities rooted in our culture — making childhood memories that connect them to Mangalore.

📖

Faith Formation & Catechism

Helping Catholic families raise their children in the faith while living in Germany. We organise occasional faith-formation sessions, sacramental preparation support, and connect families to nearby Catholic parishes with international communities.

👩‍💼

Women's Circle

A support and social network for Konkani women in Germany. Monthly meetups, discussions on work-life balance as an immigrant woman, recipe swaps, and a safe sisterhood for women navigating German life while holding onto their roots.

🏆

Youth Leadership Programme

We invest in our young adults. This programme identifies youth aged 18–30 for leadership roles in the association, event planning, and community service — ensuring the next generation carries Konkani Kutam forward with pride.

🍽️

Konkani Kitchen

Online and in-person workshops where community members share authentic Mangalorean recipes — from Chicken Ghee Roast and Fish Curry Rice to Pork Bafat, Sannas, and Rose Cookies. Preserving our food culture, one recipe at a time.

📰

Community Newsletter

A quarterly digital newsletter in Konkani and English, featuring community news, event recaps, member spotlights, tips for life in Germany, and a children's corner — staying connected even when members are spread across the country.
