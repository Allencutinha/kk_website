About Konkani Kutam

Who we are, what we stand for, and the people who make this community thrive.

Our Story

Faith Culture Language Community Family Heritage
🎯

Our Mission

To unite Konkani Catholics residing in Germany under one umbrella, preserving our faith, culture, and language while supporting one another in building a fulfilling life in Europe.

🌟

Our Vision

A thriving Konkani diaspora community in Germany where every generation — from grandparents to grandchildren — feels a proud, living connection to our Mangalorean identity.

⚖️

Our Values

Inclusivity, respect, faith, volunteerism, and the Mangalorean spirit of hospitality — aamogelan. Every member is family.

Our Committee

Volunteers who give their time, heart, and energy to keep our community alive and thriving.
