A Word from Fr. Robert Rego

Konkani Kutam was born out of a simple but profound desire — that no Konkani Catholic in Germany should ever feel alone. Far from the shores of Konkan, far from the familiar sounds of our language and the warmth of our celebrations, it is easy to feel adrift. This community exists to be your anchor.

We are not just an association. We are a family gathered in faith, bound by culture, and sustained by the love we carry for our roots. Whether you join us for Monthi Fest, a language class, or simply a shared meal, you are coming home. I pray that Konkani Kutam continues to be a place where every generation — from grandparents to grandchildren — finds belonging, joy, and pride in who we are.

— Fr. Robert Rego

Our Mission

To unite Konkani Catholics residing in Germany under one umbrella, preserving our Culture, and language while supporting one another in building a fulfilling life in Germany.

🌟

Our Vision

A thriving Konkani diaspora community in Germany where every generation from grandparents to grandchildren, feels a proud, living connection to our Konkani identity.

⚖️

Our Values

Inclusivity, respect, faith, volunteerism, and the Mangalorean spirit of hospitality — aamogelan. Every member is family.
