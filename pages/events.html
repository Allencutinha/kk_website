Monthi Fest — Our Grandest Celebration

The Feast of Our Lady of the Nativity (8 September) is the most beloved festival of Mangalorean Catholics. At Konkani Kutam, it is our biggest annual event — a full day of Holy Mass, cultural performances, traditional food, and the timeless ritual of the first harvest offering. Families come from across Germany to celebrate together.
