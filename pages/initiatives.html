Our Planned Initiatives

Beyond events, we run year-round programmes that nurture our culture, support our families, and strengthen our community.

🗣️

Konkani Language Classes

Annual online classes for children and adults. Basic conversational Konkani because every generation should be able to speak the language of their ancestors. Taught by volunteer teachers from within our community.

🎵

Cultural Programmes & Arts

Dance, drama, music, and poetry in Konkani. We provide a platform to exhibit traditional Konkani folk dances, Konkani folk songs (Mando, Dulpod), and stage performances at our annual events keeping our performing arts alive in Germany.

🤝

Newcomer Welcome Programme

Just arrived in Germany? We provide a cheat sheet for the for first few months describing the rules and responsibilities after landing in Germany

👶

Konkani Kids Club

A dedicated space for children of our community to meet, play, and grow up knowing their heritage. Monthly gatherings with storytelling, crafts, Konkani rhymes, and activities rooted in our culture — making childhood memories that connect them to Mangalore.

👩‍💼

Women's Circle

A support and social network for Konkani women in Germany. Monthly meetups, discussions on work-life balance as an immigrant woman, recipe swaps, and a safe sisterhood for women navigating German life while holding onto their roots.

🍽️

Konkani Kitchen

Online workshops where community members share authentic Mangalorean recipesfrom Chicken Ghee Roast and Fish Curry Rice to Pork Bafat, Sannas, and Rose Cookies. Preserving our food culture, one recipe at a time.
