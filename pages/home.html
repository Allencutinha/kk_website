Deu boro dis dium! Whether you moved to Germany last month or have called it home for years, you are not alone. Konkani Kutam is your Konkani family in Germany, a warm community that celebrates our shared roots, supports one another through life abroad, and ensures our children grow up proud of who we are.

✦

Why Konkani Kutam?

Because home isn't just a place it's a feeling. We recreate that feeling right here in Germany.

🏡

A Community Away From Home

Connect with fellow Konkani people across Germany — share meals, stories, and the comfort of people who truly understand where you come from.

🙏

Faith & Traditions

We celebrate the traditions of our Catholic faith together — Monthi Fest, Christmas, Easter — just as our families do back in Mangalore.

🗣️

Konkani Language & Culture

Konkani classes, cultural programmes, and a community that actively uses the language — so our children know and love their mother tongue.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Support Network

Newcomer assistance, school guidance, integration tips, and a trusted network of families who've been in your shoes.

🍱

Food & Festivities

From pork bafat and neer dosa to sorpotel and rose cookies — we come together over the food that reminds us of home.

🌍

The Best of Both Worlds

Embrace life in Germany while staying proudly Mangalorean. We help our community thrive in Europe without losing their roots.

✦
"Wherever a Kodialgar is, home is never far, as long as we are together."
— Konkani Kutam, Germany
